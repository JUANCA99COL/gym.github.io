Club K Squash

Squash is huge at Club K. Come in for a casual game, join our Big leagues, or come to our Squash club on Sunday afternoons. Memberships and Pre-Paid Bookings available. We have eight squash courts at Club K, gear is available for hire, and coaching for all levels. Permanent Bookings are good if you play regularly (e.g. every week). You have a court reserved for you each week – same time, same place. This guarantees the court will be available for you when you want it. The court must be paid for one week in advance. If you don’t want to, or cannot play one week then call us before noon on your booking day – we’ll cancel your booking for that day and move your money onto the next week.
